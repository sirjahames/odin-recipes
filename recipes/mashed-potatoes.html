Description

Mashed potato or mashed potatoes, colloquially known as mash,
is a dish made by mashing boiled or steamed potatoes, usually
with added milk, butter, salt, and pepper. It is generally
served as a side dish to meat or vegetables. Roughly mashed
potatoes are sometimes called smashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes.
  2. Heat the milk.
  3. Mash the potatoes.
